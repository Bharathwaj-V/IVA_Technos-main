HC DOZER PERFORMANCE TEST RIG

The HC Dozer Performance Test Rig is a specialized setup designed to evaluate and optimize system performance by simulating real-world conditions. It measures the flow rate of the dosing valve by modulating fuel through the rail assembly at varying pressures (up to 24 bar), while automatically controlling valves, monitoring fuel temperature and pressure, and generating test reports for specific component part numbers.

POWERFUL DIAGNOSTIC FEATURES

Engine Performance Monitoring

Measures engine output, torque, and efficiency under controlled conditions to optimize performance.

Transmission Analysis

Monitors gear shifts and transmission efficiency, detecting faults in real-time under load conditions.

Temperature & Vibration Monitoring

Records engine and component temperatures and vibrations to ensure system reliability and safety.

Fuel Consumption Analysis

Monitors fuel usage under different operating conditions to improve efficiency and reduce emissions.

Data Logging & Analytics

Records all performance metrics, generating reports to optimize system design and functionality.

Control & Remote Monitoring

Allows engineers to remotely access, monitor, and control tests in real-time through software interfaces.
