COMPRESSOR CONTROLLER

A compressor controller is an electronic or electromechanical device that manages and regulates the operation of a compressor. Its main purpose is to ensure the compressor runs efficiently, safely, and reliably under varying load and environmental conditions.

POWERFUL DIAGNOSTIC FEATURES

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuously track pressure, temperature, and motor performance for reliable operation.

Automatic Load Control

Optimizes compressor load/unload cycles to save energy and reduce wear.

Advanced Alarm System

Instant alerts for high temperature, low oil, or abnormal pressure conditions.

Data Logging

Stores operating history, fault records, and energy consumption reports.

System Protection

Safeguards compressor from overloads, overheating, and voltage fluctuations.

Remote Connectivity

Enable remote monitoring and control through secure IoT and network systems.
